    • About Me!

    Helloo!! I'm Lyanz Jerome L. Navarette. I'm a self-taught front-end and back-end developer that loves making designs that are both useful and engaging. For me, coding is a combination of logic and creativity, and I like creating projects that challenge my knowledge. In addition to my passion for development, I am an expert photographer who uses each image to tell a story and capture distinctive viewpoints. Beyond stills, dynamic scenarios and angles that give images life are what really pique my interest in cinematography. You may also frequently find me with my guitar, performing and creating music that reflects my creative spirit, when I'm not in front of the camera or buried in code. My identity is defined by these hobbies, which also motivate me to study and create every day.
